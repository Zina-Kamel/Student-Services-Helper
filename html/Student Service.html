Booking

Welcome to the room booking page

Please choose an option:


->In the join a session section, you will see all the public sessions available.You can also filter or search for the session you are willing to join.

->Once you choose your desired session you can find a under each session a join button. After clicking you will be redirected to a confirmation page and an email will be sent to you.

->In the book a room section you will be reserving an empty classroom.

->You will fill a form giving more informations about the classroom you want to register.In addition you will be able to check for the available empty classrooms.And you can also decide if you want your reservation to be private or public.
